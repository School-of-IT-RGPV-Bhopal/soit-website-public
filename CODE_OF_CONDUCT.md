# Code of Conduct

> **SoIT Student Council · Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal**

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of the SoIT website pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, religion, or sexual orientation.

We are a student-led initiative. We expect all participants to treat each other with the same professionalism they would bring to a college or workplace setting.

---

## Our Standards

**Expected of all contributors:**

- Use inclusive, welcoming, and respectful language in all interactions.
- Be constructive when giving or receiving feedback on code, design, or ideas.
- Accept differing viewpoints gracefully; focus on technical merit, not personal preference.
- Credit others' work appropriately — do not plagiarise or claim others' contributions.
- Disclose any conflicts of interest relevant to your contributions.
- Respect the time and effort of maintainers and other contributors.

**Not acceptable under any circumstances:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Sexualised language, imagery, or unwanted attention toward any person.
- Trolling, deliberately inflammatory comments, or sustained bad-faith criticism.
- Publishing private information of any person without explicit consent (doxxing).
- Submitting plagiarised, malicious, or intentionally broken code.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional or academic setting.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests, commit messages, code comments, and any other communication channel associated with this repository. It also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Violations should be reported privately to the Council Lead via the official council email listed in [README.md](./README.md). All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. Do not report violations in public Issues or PR comments.

### Response

The Council will review all reports and may take any of the following actions depending on severity:

| Severity | Action |
|---|---|
| First minor violation | Private warning with explanation |
| Repeated or moderate violation | Temporary restriction from contributing |
| Severe or deliberate violation | Permanent ban from the repository |

The Council's decision on enforcement is final. Retaliating against someone who has made a good-faith report is itself a violation of this Code of Conduct.

### What We Won't Tolerate in Reports

- Bad-faith or false reports intended to harm another contributor
- Using the reporting process as a tool for harassment
